Privacy Policy for CoExpendit™

Effective Date: March 12, 2026

1. Introduction

CoExpendit™ ("the App"), developed by Cabán Creatives Group™ ("we," "us," or "the Developer"), is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains our "Privacy-by-Design" architecture, where your data stays under your direct control within your Google ecosystem.

2. Data Ownership & Storage

CoExpendit™ does not maintain a central database or server to store your personal financial records.

  • Google Sheets: All financial data (expenses, categories, dates) is stored directly in a Google Sheet within your personal Google Drive account.
  • Local Caching: To provide offline functionality and a responsive user interface, the App stores a local cache of your spreadsheet data on your Android device. This data is managed by the Android OS and is not transmitted to the Developer.

3. Information We Access & Process

  • Google Account Information: We access your email address and profile name via Google OAuth to identify your account and facilitate collaborative features.
  • Google Drive/Sheets Permissions: The App requests permission to read, create, and edit specific Google Sheets. This is required for the App’s core functionality.
  • Collaborator Data: If you use Google’s native sharing features to invite others to a sheet, your email address and profile name will be visible to those collaborators within the App’s Profile section.

4. Data Transmission

Data is transmitted directly between your device and Google’s servers via the Google Sheets API using industry-standard encryption (HTTPS). No financial data is intercepted, stored, or processed by Cabán Creatives Group™ infrastructure.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Because your data is stored in your Google Drive, you have total control over its deletion. You may delete your data by deleting the specific Google Sheet or by revoking the App's access via your Google Account security settings.

6. Third-Party Services

The App relies on Google Play Services and the Google Sheets API. Their respective privacy policies apply to the data handled by their platforms.
